Developed an AI-powered invoice automation agent using n8n that streamlines the invoicing process for businesses. The system allows users to fill out a simple form with the sold products and client email addresses. Once submitted, the agent automatically.

• Retrieves product prices from a connected Google Sheet.

• Calculates totals, taxes, and any necessary adjustments.
